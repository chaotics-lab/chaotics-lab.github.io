FAQ

Quick answers

The essentials about what iiSU is and where it's headed.

iiSU is a visuals-first emulation frontend for Android, designed to give your retro game library a console-like presentation with rich box art, icons, and theming — without sacrificing speed or control.
iiSU is in public alpha. Core navigation, library scraping, theming, and achievement tracking are live, with online and social features actively being built out.
Yes — iiSU will remain fully free. There are no Pro tiers or paywalls; development is community-funded through voluntary support.
iiSU is a frontend, not an emulator. Connect your own ROMs and preferred emulators, and iiSU organizes everything into one polished library. iiSU does not ship with games and does not support pirated ROMs.

Philosophy

Why build iiSU?

Three ideas guide every decision — visuals, ownership, and play.

// library

I heard there's games?

Browse and launch your favorite games with beautiful visuals and intuitive controls. Connect your own ROMs and emulators, and iiSU organizes your library automatically using pristine assets and icons bundled with the app.

iiSU does not come with any games, and does not support the use of pirated ROMs.

// achievements

What about cheevos?

Track your progress and share it with friends via RetroAchievements — a core part of iiSU that gives every game an extra goal to chase, no matter how old it is.
